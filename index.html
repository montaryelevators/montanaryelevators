Our Services

Supply and Installation of Elevators

Home Elevators: Compact, stylish, and space-efficient lifts designed for private homes.

Commercial Elevators: Heavy-duty lifts for offices, malls, and other commercial buildings.

Customizable Elevators: Tailored solutions for unique architectural requirements, including panoramic glass lifts and premium interiors.

24/7 Maintenance Services

Emergency Repairs: Round-the-clock response to breakdowns or issues, with quick deployment of technicians.

Preventive Maintenance: Monthly, quarterly, or yearly checkups to ensure smooth operation.

Remote Troubleshooting: IoT-enabled systems to remotely diagnose and fix smart elevator issues.

Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

Regular inspection, cleaning, lubrication, and component replacement at no extra charge under the AMC.

Comprehensive system performance tests for safety and efficiency.

Upgradation and Modernization

Technology Upgrades: Upgrading existing elevators with the latest technology such as touchless operation, AI fault detection, and energy-saving systems.

Interior Design Updates: Enhancing cabin aesthetics with new flooring, lighting, and panel designs.

Safety Checks and Compliance

Routine safety audits to ensure elevators meet all regulatory standards.

Free annual safety inspection reports for peace of mind.

Customer Support Services

24/7 helpline for complaints and assistance. Mobile app for service requests and maintenance tracking.

Online portal for AMC renewal and payment options.

Installation of Advanced Features

Touchless controls, voice-activated commands, and battery backups for uninterrupted operation during power outages.

Energy Efficiency Solutions

Retrofitting elevators with energy-efficient motors and lighting systems, with solar-powered options available for homes and small buildings.
